## on voice

we are the 99, and we build for each other.

you believe it's important to have a voice, use this voice for purpose,
to elevate underrepresented principles & people. 

in an age of "content", misinformation, and most especially ever-growing
opportunities to collaborate with others also chasing after a better world -

## on beliefs

humanity is the core of everything - we must be unwavering in it's
protection.

industrial and technical advances have enabled deep discovery of cultures,
medical and environmental leaps in understanding and resources, and exposure
to atrocities committed often and without as much as a second thought - all 
around the world (yes, round - as in, a globe). we have the opportunity to
establish a new baseline of life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness - one
which doesn't exclude humans in any concievable way (even inconcievable ways), 
doesn't allow for hunger or ailments to be a source of worry. achieving this
_without_ technology is an insurmountable task, but it's a pursuit worth 
making. (ugh, the internal struggle seeing naked returns while honoring 80 
character wrapping)

support and understanding are our foundation, platforms have risen from a place
of people helping others achieve their own goals in a trusted and unified way.

technical depth is your grease - allowing us to sustain progression in thought
and communication.

communication is divided by dialect in varying ways - even distinct definitions
of "professional". learn the dialects and tailor your message to your audience.
some might call this masking - in many ways, it is. i'd propose an alternative
line for the little grey cells to take - we can meet people where they are. we
can bring comfort in a world of chaos, with bloody WORDS. do not squander more
than you must.
