> an unexamined life is not worth living

## on becoming

resist the solution. understand the problem. its scope. its weight.

growth isn't forged in the abstract. it is built with intention, with tangible
experiences - real systems, real people, real failure.

you can read the map. you still have to walk the ground.

errors of the mind are endless; they multiply as they travel.

every institution, every deep end, every encounter with the unfiltered weight
of being human - none of it was detour. it was formation.

show up. be present. the map didn't come first. the ground did.

## on voice

we are the 99, and we build for each other.

you believe it's important to have a voice, use this voice for purpose, to elevate underrepresented principles & people.

in an age of "content", misinformation, and most especially ever-growing opportunities to collaborate with others also chasing after a better world - silence allows for distrust, uncomfortable interactions, and guilt to fester.

## on beliefs

humanity is the core of everything - we must be unwavering in it's protection.

industrial and technical advances have enabled deep discovery of cultures, medical and environmental leaps in understanding and resources, and exposure to atrocities committed often and without as much as a second thought - all around the world (yes, round - as in, a globe). we have the opportunity to establish a new baseline of life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness - one which doesn't exclude humans in any concievable way (even inconcievable ways), doesn't allow for hunger or ailments to be a source of worry. achieving this _without_ technology is an insurmountable task, but it's a pursuit worth making. (ugh, the internal struggle seeing naked returns while honoring 80 character wrapping)

support and understanding are our foundation, platforms have risen from a place of people helping others achieve their own goals in a trusted and unified way.

technical depth is your grease - allowing us to sustain progression in thought and communication.

communication is divided by dialect in varying ways - even distinct definitions of "professional". learn the dialects and tailor your message to your audience. some might call this masking - in many ways, it is. i'd propose an alternative line for the little grey cells to take - we can meet people where they are. we can bring comfort in a world of chaos, with bloody WORDS. do not squander more than you must.
